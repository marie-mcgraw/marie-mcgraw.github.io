Recent Bakes

A few recent highlights from my kitchen

Holiday Cookies 2020

This year's cookie collection included over 20 different cookies, both classics (ginger cookies, Russian tea cakes, cut-out cookies) and new favorites (ube crinkle cookies, coffee bean shortbread rye shortbread). Full list of cookies here.

Holiday Treats 2020

A few other treats from December 2020 (clockwise from top left)--lussekatte with cinnamon filling (mashup between Linda @ Call Me Cupcake and Will's mom's Swedish cookbook recipe); homemade marshmallows; chocolate mint thumbprints, chocolate mint macarons, and lussekatte with almond paste filling (via Will's mom's recipe again); and sausage rolls with homemade puff pastry.

Cardamom Swiss Roll

Cardamom Swiss roll with espresso chocolate French buttercream. A result of watching too much Great British Bake Off. Inspired by Chetna and Nancy from S5--rolling it up in a towel was clutch!

Kayleigh Cakes

Somehow I have managed to capture Kayleigh's last 3 birthday cakes, but not many other birthday cakes. Bottom left is chai; right side is pumpkin spice latte (from two angles); top left may involve rhubarb. My piping skills have improved but my color mixing needs work

Easter Tsoureki

My attempt at a Greek Easter bread, tsoureki. Not entirely authentic as I could not track down any mastica or mahleb (this was April 2020, hunting for a hard-to-find ingredient felt like a poor choice). Eggs were naturally dyed, just for fun.

Charoset Tart

A Passover-inspired tart with charoset filling and coconut pastry cream. The tart crust is my favorite recipe from Bouchon Bakery (so not actually Passover-friendly).
