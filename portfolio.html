Recent Bakes

A few recent highlights from my kitchen

Holiday Cookies 2020

This year's cookie collection included over 20 different cookies, both classics (ginger cookies, butter spritz cookies, Russian tea cakes, cut-out cookies, thumbprints) and new favorites (ube crinkle cookies, Swedish dream cookies, coffee bean shortbread, rye shortbread, chocolate juniper sables).

Holiday Treats 2020

A few other treats from December 2020 (clockwise from top left)--Lussekatte with cinnamon filling (mashup between Linda @ Call Me Cupcake and Will's mom's Swedish cookbook recipe); homemade marshmallows (peppermint and whiskey); lussekatte with almond paste and vanilla filling (via Will's mom's recipe again); and sausage rolls with homemade puff pastry.

Cardamom Swiss Roll

Cardamom Swiss roll with espresso chocolate French buttercream. Made after watching too much Great British Bake Off.

Easter Tsoureki

My attempt at a Greek Easter bread, tsoureki. Not entirely authentic as I could not track down any mastica (this was April 2020, hunting for a hard-to-find ingredient felt like a poor choice).
