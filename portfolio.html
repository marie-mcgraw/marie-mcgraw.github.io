Recent Bakes

A few recent highlights from my kitchen

Holiday Cookies 2020

This year's cookie collection included over 20 different cookies, both classics (ginger cookies, Russian tea cakes, cut-out cookies) and new favorites (ube crinkle cookies, coffee bean shortbread rye shortbread). Full list of cookies here.

Holiday Treats 2020

A few other treats from December 2020 (clockwise from top left)--Lussekatte with cinnamon filling (mashup between Linda @ Call Me Cupcake and Will's mom's Swedish cookbook recipe); homemade marsh,mallows (peppermint and whiskey); chocolate peppermint thumbprints, chocolate peppermint macarons, and lussekatte with almond paste and vanilla filling (via Will's mom's recipe again); and sausage rolls with homemade puff pastry.

Cardamom Swiss Roll

Cardamom Swiss roll with espresso chocolate French buttercream. Made after watching too much Great British Bake Off. Inspired by Chetna and Nancy from S5--rolling it up in a towel was clutch!

Kayleigh Cakes

Somehow I manage to capture Kayleigh's birthday cakes (but no one else's?). Bottom left is chai; right side is pumpkin spice latte (two angles); top left may involve rhubarb.

Easter Tsoureki

My attempt at a Greek Easter bread, tsoureki. Not entirely authentic as I could not track down any mastica (this was April 2020, hunting for a hard-to-find ingredient felt like a poor choice). Eggs were naturally dyed, just for fun.

Charoset Tart

A Passover-inspired tart with apple-walnut filling and coconut cream.
